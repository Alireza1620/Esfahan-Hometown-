Haleem Bademjan

Intro

Haleem Bademjan is the name of an Iranian food that consists of curd, eggplant and some other additives. This dish is usually eaten with bread.

Recipe

We peel the eggplant and sprinkle it with salt, and after an hour when its bitterness is removed, we wash it with cold water and let it drain. Fry the eggplant in oil on low heat. Remove the eggplant from the pan and fry the onion in the remaining oil. Then we fry the garlic a little. Add turmeric and fry dry mint in it. After the mint is fried, they return the eggplant to the pan and pour a little water, cover the pan and put it on a gentle fire for half an hour until it softens. We can also beat it to make it uniform. Then we add some chopped walnuts to it and decorate it with walnuts, curd, onion and garlic.
